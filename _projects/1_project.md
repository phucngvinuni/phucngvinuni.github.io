An intelligent aquaculture monitoring and management system integrating IoT sensors and AI technologies for sustainable resource management, developed as part of research at the Intelligence, Computing and Communication Lab (ICCL), VinUniversity.

**Key Features:**
- Real-time water quality monitoring (pH, Temperature, NH₃, NO₂, DO)
- Computer vision pipeline for fish tracking and underwater size estimation
- Image segmentation for health and behavior analysis
- Semantic communication framework for efficient data transmission
- Mobile and web alerts for anomaly detection

**Technologies:** Python, PyTorch, OpenCV, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, IoT

**GitHub:** [AquaSense](https://github.com/korobn0608/aquasense)
